About VJCET

Nurturing Engineers, Inspiring Innovators

Established in 1994 by the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), Viswajyothi College of Engineering and Technology (VJCET) is a premier engineering institution located in the serene campus of Vazhakulam, Muvattupuzha, Kerala.

With a legacy spanning over three decades, VJCET has consistently produced graduates who excel in global technology companies, startups, and research institutions. Our NAAC 'A' grade accreditation is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality.
